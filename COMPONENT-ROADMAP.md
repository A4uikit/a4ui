# Component status

## Stable

Mature, widely-used primitives, forms, and feedback components.

- Accordion, Alert, AlertDialog, Avatar, Badge, Breadcrumb, Button
- Card (CardContent, CardHeader, CardTitle), Checkbox, Combobox, ContextMenu
- Dropdown, HoverCard, Input, Kbd, Modal, Meter, NumberInput
- Pagination, Popover, Progress, RadioGroup, SegmentedControl, Separator
- Skeleton, Slider, Spinner, Switch, Table (TableBody, TableCell, TableHead, TableHeadCell, TableRow)
- Tabs, Textarea, Toggle, ToggleGroup, Tooltip

Newer, complex, and data-heavy components; stable but feature-rich.

- Affix, Anchor, AvatarGroup, BackToTop, BottomNavigation
- Calendar, CalendarHeatmap, Carousel, Cascader, Clock, Collapse, ColorPicker
- Command, Comment, Countdown, DataGrid, DateField, Descriptions
- Dropzone, Empty, FloatingActionButton, Highlight, Image, List
- Marquee, Mentions, NotificationCenter, PageHeader, Rating, Result
- RingProgress, Sortable, SpeedDial, Splitter, Stat, Stepper
- TagInput, Timeline, Tour, Transfer, Tree, TreeSelect, VirtualList

## Layout & Theming

- AppShell, EffectsToggle, NavGroup, SnowScenery, SpaceBackground
- ThemedScenery, ThemeToggle, ChristmasBackground
- Themes: space, dino, doctor, scientist, soccer, snow, christmas
